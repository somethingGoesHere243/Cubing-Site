First Two Layers

White Cross

We begin by solving the white cross. This is a fairly intuitive process as we don't need to worry about ruining any previous work.

Solved Cross

It is important to ensure the white edges end up in the correct positions around the white center.

White Corners

We now finish the first layer by correctly inserting the white corners into the white layer.

Below are the 3 possible cases for inserting the white-blue-red corner:

R U R'

F' U' F

F' L' U2 L F

Second Layer

We now complete the second layer by inserting the 4 edges into their respective slots.

Below are the 2 possible cases for inserting the blue-red edge:

U R U R' U' F' U' F

U' F' U' F U R U R'

If an edge piece is already in the second layer but is postioned wrong we must first remove it by placing it on the right side of the front face and performing either of the 2 above algorirthms.
